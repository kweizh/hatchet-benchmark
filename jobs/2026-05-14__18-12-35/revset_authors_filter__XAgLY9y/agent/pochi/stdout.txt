# Filter Commits by Author and Description with a jj Revset ## Background Jujutsu (`jj`) provides a powerful functional revset language for selecting commits. Two of the most useful predicates are `author(pattern)` (which matches against the author's name OR email) and `description(pattern)` (which matches against the commit description). Combined with the `&` operator and the `substring:` string-pattern kind, you can build queries that pick out very specific subsets of history — for example, "all commits by Alice that have the word 'fix' in their description".
